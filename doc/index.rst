Welcome to the Minarca Docs! 
============================

This comprehensive resource is designed to assist sysadmin in harnessing the power of Minarca, an exceptional open-source backup solution. Whether you are an IT professional or a technology enthusiast, this documentation will guide you through the process of self-hosting and utilizing Minarca to its fullest potential. With its robust features and extensive visibility into the backup process, Minarca empowers service providers to ensure the utmost reliability, security, and efficiency in data backup operations. Get ready to delve into the intricacies of this remarkable tool and unlock a world of possibilities for your backup infrastructure.
